Detailed Description

Computation of Fourier decomposition of force/torques caused by SRP over a shape model.

Author
Benjamin Bercovici

Wrapper around YORPLib to allow VTK's Polydata to be utilized as shape containers. see Scheeres, D. J. (2007). The dynamical evolution of uniformly rotating asteroids subject to YORP. Icarus, 188(2), 430â€“450. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.icarus.2006.12.015 for more details
